**Project Psyche: Build**   

**What's the Game About?**
Build a working Psyche spacecraft by putting all the pieces in the right spots. Use logic and the part descriptions to figure out where everything goes. Get it right and launch successfully. Get it wrong and... well, things get messy.


How You Play
1. **Browse Parts** \- Look through all the spacecraft parts in the catalog. Click any part to see what it does.  
2. **Place Parts** \- Drag parts onto the spacecraft. Rotate and zoom to find where they belong.  
3. **Get Feedback**  
   * Right spot: Part clicks into place with a satisfying snap. Your readiness meter goes up.  
   * Wrong spot: Part gets rejected with a red X and a hint like "needs power connection."  
4. **Keep Going** \- Try different spots until everything fits. Check your readiness score anytime.  
5. **Launch** \- When you think you're done, hit the big "INITIATE LAUNCH SEQUENCE" button and see what happens.

**The Screen Layout**

**Main View (Center)**

* Space background with the spacecraft in the middle  
* Desktop: Click and drag to rotate. Scroll to zoom. Right-click to pan.  
* Mobile: One finger to rotate. Pinch to zoom. Two fingers to pan.  
* When you drag a part to the right spot, it glows green. Wrong spots glow red.

**Parts Catalog (Left Side \- Can Hide)** Groups of parts to drag:

* Science Tools (Magnetometer, Camera, Spectrometer)  
* Radios (X-band Antenna, Laser)  
* Power Stuff (Solar Panels, Fuel, Thrusters)  
* Main Structure pieces

Click the info button to learn what each part does.

**Part Details (Right Side \- Pops Up)** When you click on a part, you see:

* What it's called  
* What it does  
* Where it should go (hints\!)  
* What it needs to work

**Flight Readiness Bar (Top)**

* Big percentage that goes from 0% to 100%  
* Icons for Power, Radio, Science, and Structure (turn green when correct)  
* Launch button (grayed out until you hit 95%+)

How a Game Might Go
**Story Board**

**Starting Out** You see the empty spacecraft frame. All the parts are listed on the left. Readiness is at 0%. No instructions telling you what to do first \- just figure it out.

**Trying Things** You drag the Magnetometer to a solar panel. Red X \- nope\! You check the info: "needs to be on a boom away from other magnetic stuff." You find the boom, snap it in, and boom \- 8% ready\!

**The Lightbulb Moment** Can't figure out where the laser goes. You try next to the antenna \- rejected. Check the specs: needs a clear view to Earth. Oh\! It goes on top pointing away from the spacecraft. Click. The radio icon turns green. Progress\!

**Almost There** Ship's at 98%. Launch button is glowing. But the power icon is still yellow. You spot it \- forgot to connect the solar panel cable\! Add the power coupling. 100%\! "All Systems Good. Ready to go."

**Success** Hit launch. Countdown starts. 3... 2... 1... Rocket blasts off\! The spacecraft separates, solar panels unfold, and it cruises into space. "Mission Success\!" Triumphant music plays.

**Failure** 
Launched at 70% ready? The rocket sputters and fails. Or it lifts off but explodes. Or it makes it to space but spins out of control. Screen says what went wrong: "Missing communication system" or "Bad weight balance." Back to the workshop to fix it. Try again\!
